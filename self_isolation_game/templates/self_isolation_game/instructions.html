Instructions

You are given {{ Constants.endowment }} for every round (called endowment). Every participant can choose how much to stay home (self-isolate) in order to limit the spread of the virus. Self-isolation costs points, for example: moderate self-isolation costs 20% of that round's points and complete self-isolation costs 40% of that round's points. If your group is in lockdown, everybody only earns a fraction of that round's points.

Each round, you will first be asked how much you would self-isolate if you knew how much the others in your group would self-isolate that round. Then you will predict what others will do in that round. You will also be asked how much you will actually self-isolate (but you won't know how much others are self-isolating). Your endowment in each round will depend on whether you are in lockdown, but is otherwise 100 points. Each round's payoff equals your endowment, minus the amount you contribute by self-isolating.

Please do your best to answer each question because we cannot pay you for rounds where you didn't answer every question.
