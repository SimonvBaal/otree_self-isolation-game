Instructions

You are given a variable number of points for every round (called endowment). Every participant can choose how much to stay home (self-isolate) in order to limit the spread of the virus. Self-isolation costs points, for example: moderate self-isolation costs 20% of that round's points and complete self-isolation costs 40% of that round's points. If your group is in lockdown, everybody only earns 30% of that round's points.

Each round, you will first be asked how much you would self-isolate if you knew how much the others in your group would self-isolate that round.
